Pay With Your Phone, Ride the Bus

Use Token Transit to buy your pass. Pay with your credit, debit, or commuter benefits card and always have your pass with you. Ride the bus anytime with ease and convenience.

Ride With Token Transit

1. Purchase

Select any fare tye you like, from a single ride to a monthly pass.

2. Activate

When you are ready to ride, tap your pass to activate. Your phone holds all your passes.

3. Ride

As you board the bus, show the driver your digital ticket.
